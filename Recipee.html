My favourite Meal

The name of my favourite meal is Yam porridge . It is a nutritious and a balanced diet. It is a common delicacy in most Nigerian homes as yam is a staple food in nigeria and africa at large.

About Yam

Yam is a root tuber of African and Potugeese Origin. Its botanical name is Diascorea spp. Yam contains mainly carbohydrate and some mineral salts and vitamins. It is also a vegetable. It is usually eaten in combination with sauce or in porridge form.

Ingredients for Preparing yam porridge

INGREDIENTS
MEASUREMENTS
Salt 1 teaspoonful
Red oil 50 mls
Yam 1 medium-sized tuber
Leafy vegetables Desired Quantity
Seasoning 3 cubes
Water 500mls
Crayfish 1/2 cup

Steps to Prepare Yam porridge:

  • Peel the skin of the yam
  • Cut the yam into smaller and desired sizes
  • Grind/blend your crayfish, freshpepper
  • Wash and slice your onions and leafy green vegetables
  • Wash the peeled and diced yam, put them in a pot and add about 500mls of water.
  • Put the pot on the fire and add onion, salt, seasoning cube, pepper and crayfish
  • Allow to boil for about 20 minutes, add the sliced vegetables and red oil
  • Allow to boil for another minute, bring the food down to serve
